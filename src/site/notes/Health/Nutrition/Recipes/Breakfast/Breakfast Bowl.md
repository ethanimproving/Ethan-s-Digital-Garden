# Ingredients

Fried oats (6 cups)
1 butter stick (8 oz.)
Honey (5-8 tablespoons)
Shredded almonds (optional)
Cut up banana
Fresh blueberries
Milk

# Directions

1) Put oats over medium heat
2) Let butter stick melt atop the oats while you stir constantly
3) Put in bowl
4) Top with remaining ingredients

## Comments

8 tbsp = 0.5 cup
