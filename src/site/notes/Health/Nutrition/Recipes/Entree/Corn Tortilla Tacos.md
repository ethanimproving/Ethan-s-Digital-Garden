# Ingredients

Corn tortilla
Refried beans
Shredded cheese
Onions
Green pepper
Avocado dip
Organic tortilla chips
Sour cream
Ground meat

# Directions

1) Warm refried beans and ground meat
2) Warm tortillas in paper towel covered by two pans in toaster oven
3) Shred cheese
4) Cut onions and green pepper
5) Mash avocado flesh
